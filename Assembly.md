

## Step 1: Motor plate, frame and oDrive

| Item                           | Qty  |
| ------------------------------ | ---- |
| Zylinderkopfschraube M3x16     | 6    |
| Stoppmutter M3                 | 6    |
| Distanzscheibe d3x1 Kunststoff | 6    |

Note: Do not tighten the connection with force. We do not want to break the oDrive PCB.



The alloy motor plate is not symmetrical, it is important that its round inner edge is on the left side as shown here. There two holes are required so the power cable on the oDrive have enough free space around them as isolation.

The motor plate is placed on top of the frame and all its six holes get screws from the underside. On top of that are 1mm thick plastic insulators. These 1mm make sure the power stages of the oDrive are in direct contact with the motor plate for better heat dissipation. Thermally conductive paste might be placed there for even better contact. Only needed in high power operations.
